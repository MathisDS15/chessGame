Rules

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on an 8x8 grid. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king.

Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 knights, 2 bishops, and 8 pawns.

The game can end in checkmate, stalemate, or draw.

How to Play

To move a piece, click on it and then click on the destination square.

Each type of piece has its own movement rules. For example, rooks move in straight lines, while bishops move diagonally.

Try to control the center of the board and protect your pieces!
